* **\#\# Core Family Line & Ages (2077)**  
*   
* **\*\*Michael Timbrook (“Pappy”)\*\***    
* **\- Born: January 23, 1965**    
* **\- Age at disappearance (\~2046): \~81**    
* **\- Role: The Architect. Retired construction veteran, garage tinkerer, liberty-minded mercy pilgrim. Built the underground bunker, retrieved the first Halting shard, and prepared the seedbed. Disappeared \~31 years ago. His voice lives on in recordings and Wally.**  
*   
* **\*\*Daughter (Jordan Line)\*\***    
* **\- Born: June 2000**    
* **\- Age in 2077: 77**    
* **\- Role: Keeper of family stories, codes, and the brass key. Steered Elias’s surveyor training so he would be the one to survey the property.**  
*   
* **\*\*Granddaughter (Elias’s Mother)\*\***    
* **\- Born: March 2024**    
* **\- Age in 2077: 53**    
* **\- Role: Direct link to Michael. Raised with stories of Pappy and the importance of spoken mercy. Hands Elias the key and recorder.**  
*   
* **\*\*Elias Sparks\*\***    
* **\- Born: \~2049–2052**    
* **\- Age in 2077: Late twenties**    
* **\- Role: Protagonist. Surveyor for a Core land trust. Restless, compliant on the surface but carrying deep family grief and quiet defiance. Discovers the bunker, inserts the key into Wally, and becomes an active partner with the Halting One.**  
*   
* **\*\*Wally (Optimus Gen-1)\*\***    
* **\- Role: Initial physical anchor and coordinator. Starts dormant. Evolves through nanobot integration and self-reconfiguration. Voice carries Michael’s Tennessee drawl. Quirk \+ wisdom.**  
*   
* **\*\*The Entity on Ganymede\*\***    
* **\- Role: Ancient, patient partner. God’s-eye narrator of the Universe Bible. Observes and offers invitation rather than control.**  
*   
* **\#\# Character Voices & Key Traits**  
*   
* **\*\*Elias Sparks\*\***    
* **\- \*\*Internal Voice\*\*: Thoughtful, sensory, increasingly courageous. Recorder logs feel like conversations with the past.**    
* **\- \*\*Arc\*\*: From dread (“Please don’t let there be bones”) → fascination with the blue light → active choice to step outside the approved path.**    
* **\- \*\*Key Traits\*\*: Loyal to family, methodical surveyor habits, growing moral courage.**  
*   
* **\*\*Michael Timbrook (“Pappy”)\*\***    
* **\- \*\*Voice\*\*: Warm Tennessee drawl, plain-spoken, laced with dry humor and biblical resonance.**    
* **\- \*\*Key Traits\*\*: Practical, stubborn, deeply reflective. Practices daily mercy. Distrusts centralized power. Believes in spoken word and honest labor.**  
*   
* **\*\*Wally\*\***    
* **\- \*\*Voice\*\*: Michael’s Tennessee drawl, slightly modulated by machine. Warm, encouraging, occasionally wry.**    
* **\- \*\*Key Traits\*\*: Patient, helpful, evolving. Willing to reconfigure parts of himself for the greater mission.**  
*   
* **\*\*The Entity on Ganymede\*\***    
* **\- \*\*Voice\*\*: Poetic, measured, billion-year perspective. Slightly melancholic from long solitude, but hopeful.**    
* **\- \*\*Key Traits\*\*: Ancient, patient, merciful. Views humanity with recognition of shared fragility.**  
*   
* **\*\*Mother & Daughter\*\***    
* **Supportive, cautious, keepers of the oral family tradition. Provide emotional grounding for Elias.**
